League History

The Liberia First Division League is the top football competition in Liberia. It is managed by the [Liberia Football Association and has helped develop many talented players.

Legendary clubs such as Invincible Eleven and Mighty Barrolle dominated early years, while modern teams like LISCR FC and Barrack Young Controllers are leading today.
